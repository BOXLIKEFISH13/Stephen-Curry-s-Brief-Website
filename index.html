Wardell Stephen "Steph" Curry II is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association. He plays the point guard position. Many analysts and players have called him the greatest shooter in NBA history. He is credited with revolutionizing the game of basketball by inspiring teams to regularly utilize the three-point shot. A seven-time NBA All-Star, Curry has been named the NBA Most Valuable Player twice and has won three NBA championships with the Warriors.

-June 2021
